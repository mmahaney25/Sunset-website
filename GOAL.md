# GOAL: Clone the Sunset (hellosunset.com) estate settlement platform with full UI and simulated end-to-end pipeline
Build a pixel-faithful recreation of Sunset's estate settlement website (hellosunset.com) with all features functional against dummy data. The site has six core modules: (1) Search and Discovery — user uploads death certificate and answers intake questions, system discovers all financial accounts across 16 asset types (IRA, 401k, savings, checking, CD, stocks, bonds, life insurance, funeral insurance, HSA, credit cards, property, vehicles, crypto, business accounts, pensions) and all liability types (credit cards, personal loans, auto loans/leases, mortgages/HELOCs, student loans, business loans, medical debt, lines of credit, collections/charge-offs), presenting a unified deduplicated report within simulated response time. (2) Probate Document Generation — generates county-specific legal forms across all 50 states and 3,000+ counties with optional online notarization flow. (3) Estate Bank Account — creates a simulated FDIC-insured consolidation account with EIN filing flow, expense tracking, and fund collection. (4) Closure Tool — lets users notify creditors via email, fax, phone, and mail with status tracking (sent/pending/confirmed/disputed); notifications are simulated (logged, not actually sent) but the full compose/send/track UI must work. (5) Financial Transfer — manages closing and consolidating accounts into the estate account with identity and fraud check simulation. (6) Inheritance Distribution — distributes funds per will, trust, or state intestacy law with approval gates. **UI Requirements:** Homepage matches Sunset's layout: hero with "Find what they left behind" headline and "Begin Search" CTA, "Trusted by 10,000+ families" badge, 11 five-star testimonial cards with names and cities, partner logos section (Chase, Merrill, Schwab, Fidelity, USAA, etc.), three benefit cards (expert-guided, no cost, user-controlled), DIY-vs-Sunset comparison section, asset types grid showing all 16 categories, five-step process section with learn-more links, and login flow. The app dashboard shows the five-step pipeline as a visual tracker with current step highlighted. **Success Criteria:** 1. Homepage visually matches hellosunset.com layout, sections, and content flow — screenshot comparison should show equivalent structure 2. "Begin Search" flow collects deceased info (name, SSN, DOB, DOD, address) and simulates discovery returning 8-15 dummy accounts across at least 6 asset types within 3 seconds 3. Liability discovery returns 4-8 dummy debts with creditor name, balance, status, shared-debt flag, and contact info 4. Unified report view deduplicates and organizes all discovered assets and liabilities by type with totals 5. Probate document generator accepts state and county, produces at least 3 dummy legal forms appropriate to that jurisdiction 6. Estate bank account module simulates EIN application, creates account view with balance, transaction history, and expense categorization 7. Closure tool composes notifications per creditor with appropriate channel (email/fax/mail/phone), logs all sends, tracks status per account with 14/30-day follow-up flags 8. Transfer module simulates closing 3+ accounts and moving balances to estate account with fraud check gate 9. Distribution module accepts beneficiary list, calculates shares per will/intestacy rules, simulates fund transfers with approval step 10. Full pipeline demo: load a preset "demo estate" with 12 assets, 6 liabilities, 3 beneficiaries and walk through all five steps end-to-end without errors 11. Shared debt detection flags joint/co-signed accounts in the liability report 12. Responsive design works on mobile and desktop 13. All dummy data is realistic (real bank names, plausible balances, correct state/county combinations)
